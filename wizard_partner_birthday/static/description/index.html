Membership Details

This module allows you to add a person's membership details and personal details like Last name, DOB and Gender

Contacts >> Contacts >> Membership

By enabling 'Is A Member' field, you can enter the member details and car details. And you can also see the rewards.

Click the create button under the car details tab to add plate numbers. Enter the car plate number and enable 'Primary' if you want to make the car as primary. Then click Save button.

You can see the entered car details under the 'Car Details' tab.
