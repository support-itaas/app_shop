Customer Billing

Several invoices to a single customer bill.

Combine several invoices of same customer which is not yet paid to a single customer Bill.

Which helps the customer to get a summary of due payments. This will be very useful in business who follows the payment in certain duration like monthly, in two months, in quarter etc.

Create Customer Bill

Create customer bill and choose your customer, system will give you all unpaid invoices of that customer.

screenshot is the customer bill with unpaid invoices.

Register the Payment

Create a single payment against several invoices.

Register your payment as a single payment against against the selected invoices.

Partial payment will again act as normal scenario. Which reconcile the invoice whose due amount can cover from paid amount.

Print the customer bill

You can print and submit the customer bill to your customers.
